What Is Redbubble?

Redbubble is a Print-On-Demand (POD) online marketplace where independent designers and artists can showcase their talent and sell their artwork on a wide range of products. From clothing and home decor to phone cases and stickers, Redbubble allows artists to reach a global audience with their unique creations.

When a customer purchases a product with your artwork on Redbubble, the company takes care of the printing process and handles all aspects of shipping. This means that you can focus on what you do best: creating stunning designs and growing your brand.

As a seller on Redbubble, you earn a commission on each sale, known as the “artist margin.” This commission is a percentage of the total price paid by the customer, and it varies depending on the type of product sold. Redbubble provides transparency by showing the breakdown of how much you earn from each sale.

One of the significant advantages of selling on Redbubble is that it’s free and accessible to anyone. Whether you’re an experienced artist or just starting your creative journey, Redbubble offers a platform to share your work and connect with a community of like-minded individuals.

How Does Redbubble Work?

Redbubble operates on a simple and user-friendly business model:

  • Create an account: Sign up for a Redbubble account to become a seller and gain access to the platform’s features.
  • Upload your artwork: Once you’re registered, you can start uploading your designs. Redbubble supports various file formats, allowing you to showcase your work in its best quality.
  • Select products: Choose from Redbubble’s extensive product catalog and decide which items you want to feature your artwork on, such as t-shirts, prints, mugs, and more.
  • Set your prices: Determine the prices for your products, taking into consideration the production costs, market demand, and your desired earnings.
  • Market and promote: Spread the word about your Redbubble store by leveraging social media platforms, engaging with the Redbubble community, and exploring marketing opportunities.
  • Earn and receive payments: When customers purchase your products, you earn your artist margin. Redbubble handles all payment processing and transfers your earnings to your chosen payout method.

    • Selling on Redbubble provides artists with a convenient way to monetize their creativity while reaching a global audience. The platform empowers individuals to showcase their talent, connect with customers worldwide, and earn income from their passion.

    Other Print-On-Demand Options

    While Redbubble is a popular choice for selling custom merchandise, there are several other print-on-demand websites that you should consider. Each platform offers unique features and opportunities to help you reach a wider audience and increase your sales. Here are some alternative options:

    Etsy

    Etsy is a well-established online marketplace known for its focus on handmade and vintage products. It’s an excellent platform for creators who offer unique, one-of-a-kind items. With a dedicated print-on-demand section, Etsy allows you to expand your reach and tap into a larger customer base that appreciates the artistry and craftsmanship of handmade goods.

    TeeSpring

    TeeSpring is a print-on-demand platform specifically designed for creating and selling custom apparel. It offers a user-friendly interface and comprehensive marketing tools to help you promote and drive traffic to your store. TeeSpring provides options for selling a wide range of products, including t-shirts, hoodies, mugs, and more.

    Shopify

    Shopify is a popular e-commerce platform that allows you to create and customize your own online store. It gives you complete control over the buyer experience, branding, and design. With Shopify, you can integrate various print-on-demand apps and services to seamlessly manage your inventory and fulfill orders. This platform is ideal for those who want to build a unique brand and have full control over their online business.

    Merch by Amazon

    Merch by Amazon is another noteworthy print-on-demand platform with a massive customer base. It offers a straightforward process for uploading designs and selling them on various products, including t-shirts, hoodies, and other merchandise. As part of the Amazon ecosystem, Merch by Amazon provides exposure to millions of potential customers, making it a valuable platform for reaching a wide audience.

    When exploring these other print-on-demand options, carefully consider your target market, product niche, and personal preferences to determine the best fit for your business. Each platform has its own strengths and features that can help you maximize your earnings and build a successful online brand.
